Welcome to the Jang Brothers Blog!

We are two brothers dedicated towards reporting on the latest KBO news and happenings in English. Our goal is bridging the gap in modern understanding and knowledge of South Korean Baseball.

We provide weekly recaps, player spotlights, aigoos of the week, and everything spanning the KBO! Surf through our website to get your fix of KBO content!

A little about us - we are two Korean-American brothers (Lawrence and Andrew) who are die-hard KBO fans. Lawrence is a longtime follower of the Doosan Bears with a secret crush on Park Kun-Woo (박건우). On the other hand, Andrew has been stuck in purgatory with Kiwoom Heroes and a Kim Ha-Seong (김하성) stan. We hope you learn more about the KBO through our love of the game!
