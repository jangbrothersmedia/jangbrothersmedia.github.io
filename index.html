Kiwoom Heroes

The standard for small ball baseball in the KBO, the Kiwoom Heroes have used analytics to power the smallest payroll to the playoffs every year. They enter the 2020 playoffs as the 5 seed vying for their first championship ever, after a disappointing end to the regular season.

The Heart of Doosan
Jae-Won Oh

Doosan's team captain, Oh has held down the second base position for the Bears since his entry into the KBO in 2007. Oh's sure-handed fielding and power from the left side of the plate has fueled the Bears to unprecedented success in his run as captain.

LG's Living Legend
Young-Taik Park

The KBO all-time leader in hits, Park is the full embodiment of a professional baseball player. Park has hit in the three hole for LG since 2002, winning the Steal King in 2005 and the Batting Title in 2009. Having announced 2020 as his final year, his presence will be missed throughout the league.

Kiwoom's Generational Talent
Ha-Seong Kim

Kiwoom's 5 tool shortstop, Kim is headed to the MLB after the 2020 season. Kim has had 6 straight .800+ OPS seasons to combine with increasing home run numbers every year. Korean fans await his arrival to the USA, where he can hopefully open the path for even more KBO players to the MLB.
